1. DATA COLLECTION

At FCA, we value your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to process your automotive orders, including name, shipping address, and vehicle specifications for fitment verification.

2. SECURITY ARCHITECTURE

All transactions are processed through AES-256 bit encrypted channels. We do not store raw credit card data on our local servers; all payment processing is handled by Tier-1 PCI-compliant financial partners.

Information regarding your vehicle builds and specifications is used solely for improving our product recommendations and ensuring hardware compatibility. This data is never sold to third-party marketing firms.

4. YOUR RIGHTS

Under global data protection regulations, you have the right to request a full extract of your data or its permanent deletion from our hardware synchronization servers at any time.
