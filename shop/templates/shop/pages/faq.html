TECHNICAL FAQ

Everything you need to know about fitment, installation, and performance certification.

FITMENT & COMPATIBILITY

Will these parts fit my specific model year? +

Our parts are designed using OEM 3D scans. Each product page lists specific model years. If you are unsure, provide your VIN via the contact form for a precision verification.

Do you offer professional installation? +

We have a network of certified partner shops globally. For major builds (widebodies/engine mods), we recommend using one of our authorized centers to maintain warranty status.

SHIPPING & LOGISTICS

How is carbon fiber protected during shipping? +

All carbon components are vacuum-sealed, foam-wrapped, and shipped in double-walled reinforced crates to ensure zero-defect delivery.

What is the average lead time? +

In-stock items ship within 48 hours. Custom-order carbon components (forged/colored) typically have a lead time of 4-6 weeks for artisan manufacturing.

WARRANTY & RETURNS

What is your guarantee on finish quality? +

We offer a 2-year guarantee against UV yellowing and clear-coat delamination on all our carbon fiber components.
